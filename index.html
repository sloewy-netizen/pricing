How AI Brand Creations Work

What's an AI Brand Creation?

Any document you create that uses AI-powered features (AI Writing Assistant, Brand Guardian, Brand OS, Command Center, or AI Marqet Apps) and then distribute (download, print, export, or publish). Upgradeable allocations let you scale with your needs.

How Allocation Works

Your AI Brand Creation allocation is issued upfront at the start of your billing period. Unused creations expire at the end of the period—they don't roll over. On Business and Scale, you can upgrade your allocation mid-cycle in 100-unit increments ($75 each) to handle additional usage.

Usage Enforcement

80%: Early warning via in-product notification. 100%: Soft cap with alerts, but features continue. 125%: Hard cap—AI features temporarily disabled until you upgrade.
