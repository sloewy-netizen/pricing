Frequently Asked Questions

What's an AI creation?

Any piece of content your team creates using our AI tools (writing assistant, brand guardian, design AI) and then shares—whether that's downloading, printing, exporting, or publishing it.

Do I have to use AI?

Nope! You can create unlimited non-AI content. The monthly allocation only applies when you use AI features. Starter plan users can create manually without any limits.

What if I exceed my monthly allowance?

You'll get warned at 80%. At 100%, you'll get alerts but everything still works. At 125%, AI features pause temporarily. You can upgrade your allowance anytime—no contract changes needed.

Do unused creations roll over?

No. Your allocation resets each month. This keeps pricing simple and fair. If you need more, you can upgrade anytime for just $75 per 100 additional creations.

Can I upgrade mid-month?

Absolutely. Upgrade anytime and only pay for what you use. No waiting, no paperwork. Just add what you need when you need it.

Which plan should I pick?

Starter: Solo creators or freelancers. Business: Small teams just getting started with AI. Scale: High-volume production teams. Enterprise: Organizations needing custom setups.
